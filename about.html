What is frugal innovation?

Frugal innovation is about reducing the complexity and cost of a product. Simply, frugal innovation provides functional solutions through few resources for the many who have little means.

“As a process frugal innovation discovers new business models, reconfigures value chains, and redesigns products to serve users who face extreme affordability constraints, in a scalable and sustainable manner. It involves either overcoming or tapping institutional voids and resource constraints to create more inclusive markets (Bhatti, 2011).”

What we do

Inova´s goal is to provide information about frugal innovation to people. We host workshops and innovation camps for both individuals, start-ups or other companies. After attending our frugal innovation camps, you will have deep knowledge about frugal innovation and how to use frugal innovation combined with your business.
